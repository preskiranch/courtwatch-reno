## Description

Court Watch AAU helps parents, coaches, players, and fans keep up with youth basketball tournaments from their phone.

Find registered teams, follow the teams you care about, and view schedule updates, court assignments, opponent matchups, records, points leaders, final results, and official source links. The app is built for busy tournament weekends when families are moving between gyms and need fast, clear updates.

Features:

- Search registered teams by tournament.
- Follow teams for the specific event they are playing in.
- View schedules, courts, venues, opponents, and records.
- See live, upcoming, final, and tournament-finished status.
- Track points leaders and final placements by division.
- Open venue locations in maps.
- Sync followed teams across devices with an optional free account.
- Share Court Watch AAU with parents, coaches, and teammates.

Court Watch AAU is an independent companion tracker and is not affiliated with tournament operators. Official schedules and rulings come from tournament staff.

## Privacy Summary

Data the app may collect:

- Account email address, if the user creates a free sync account.
- Followed team and tournament selections.
- Device/session identifiers used to keep followed teams and active-user counts working.
- Push notification subscription data, if the user enables notifications.
- Basic operational logs needed to keep sync and alerts reliable.

Data the app does not collect:

- Player roster searches.
- Precise device location.
- Contacts.
- Photos or camera data.
- Payment information.
- Advertising tracking identifiers.

Data is not sold and is not used for cross-app advertising tracking.

## Review Test Instructions

No login is required.

1. Open the app.
2. Select a tournament.
3. Tap Teams.
4. Search for any registered team.
5. Tap Follow.
6. Return to Dashboard and Schedule to see followed-team cards.

Optional account sync can be tested from the Teams screen by creating a free account, following a team, signing in on another device, and confirming the followed team appears there too.
